Bio

As of October 2021, I'm a PhD Student in Computer Science at Université Côte D'Azur in the COATI team which is a joint project-team between Inria Sophia Antipolis - Méditerranée and the I3S laboratory, under the supervision of Frédéric Havet and Stéphane Bessy.
My main interests lie in combinatorics and theoretical computer science. In particular, I am interested in graph colouring, directed graphs, reconfiguration, extremal combinatorics and probabilistic methods applied to these areas.
